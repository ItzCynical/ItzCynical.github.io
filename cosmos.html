· Cosmos Roach ·

Project Overview

The COSMOS Summer Program at University of California, Santa Cruz had us programming the behavior of our roach bots. The goal of the project was to program the bot to move into shaded areas. It used a light sensor to detect if it was under any light and bump sensors on all four diagonals to detect if it collides with a wall. My partner and I programmed our bot to move forward until it found shade and maneuvering to the opposite direction of where it collides with a wall.
